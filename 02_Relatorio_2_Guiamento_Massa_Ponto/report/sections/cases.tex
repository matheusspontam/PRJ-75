\section{Simulated Cases}

\subsection{Case 1}

The target is \(65~\text{km}\) ahead of the launch point. The missile is launched directly toward the target. The seeker remains off during the initial pre-programmed leg and terminal guidance starts when the remaining range reaches \(20~\text{km}\).

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{Case 1 setup.}
\begin{tabular}{lc}
\toprule
Item & Value \\
\midrule
Initial target range & \(65~\text{km}\) \\
Initial bearing angle & \(0^\circ\) \\
Initial missile heading & \(0^\circ\) \\
Seeker activation criterion & \(R \leq 20~\text{km}\) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Case 2}

The target is \(15~\text{km}\) from the missile, but the missile is launched with a \(90^\circ\) heading error. The seeker is powered from launch, but terminal guidance is not allowed until the target enters the \(40^\circ\) seeker cone. The pre-programmed guidance turns the missile until that condition is satisfied.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{Case 2 setup.}
\begin{tabular}{lc}
\toprule
Item & Value \\
\midrule
Initial target range & \(15~\text{km}\) \\
Target bearing from launch point & \(0^\circ\) \\
Initial missile heading & \(90^\circ\) \\
Seeker status & On at launch \\
Terminal guidance criterion & \(|\lambda-\psi| \leq 40^\circ\) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
