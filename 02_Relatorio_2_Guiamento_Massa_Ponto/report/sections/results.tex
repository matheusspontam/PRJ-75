In Case 1, the missile flies almost straight during the pre-programmed phase and switches to terminal guidance at the required \(20~\text{km}\) range.

In Case 2, the seeker is on from launch, but terminal guidance is blocked while the target is outside the \(40^\circ\) cone. The pre-programmed turn brings the line of sight into the seeker field of view, and terminal proportional navigation then takes over.
